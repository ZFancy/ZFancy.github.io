Short Bio

I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student at Trustworthy Machine Learning and Reasoning (TMLR) Group in the Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University, advised by Dr. Bo Han. Before that, I received my B.Eng. degree of Computer Science and Technology (Top-notched Student Program) from Sichuan University in 2021.

My research interests lie in the broad areas of robust and reliable machine learning, like adversarial robustness, learning and inference with Out-of-distribution data, and other related sub-topics with corresponding applications.
